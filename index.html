Our History

MAS Intimates has established its presence for 25 years for its perfect blend of daring, creativity and visionary thinking. The three brothers Mahesh, Ajay and Sharad Amalean started the business of manufacturing lingerie in 1986 which was an unconventional thought at that time but turned out to be a successful business in this century.

Our Vision

We see a day when we clothe the world with possibility, our people and partners realise their dreams and sustainability is woven into the fabric of our industry.

Our Mission

We inspire our PEOPLE to change the world for good.
We innovate PRODUCTS that are human-centred and sustainable.
We build PARTNERSHIPS to excel and achieve ambitious profitable growth, together.

Our History

NeoSolax (Pvt) Ltd is an outsourcing company in Sri Lanka which is specialized in Magento eCommerce Development. The dream of executive Muditha Ediriweera from 2011 finally came true in May 2019, NeoSolax became a privately owned company as NeoSolax (Pvt).

Our Vission

Our goal is to accelerate the capabilities, make life convenient and efficient for our direct and indirect clients.

Our Mission

we develop user centric software by training our engineers not only in the technology but the consumer behaviors and customer psychology.
